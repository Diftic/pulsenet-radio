Stream PulseNet with OBS Studio

  1. In OBS, add a Window Capture source.
  2. Set Window to PulseNet Player.
  3. Set Capture Method to Windows 10 (1903 and up).
  4. Tick Capture Audio (BETA) to bring the player's audio into the broadcast.
  5. Untick Capture Cursor to keep your mouse out of the broadcast.
  6. Tick Client Area.

Press F9 to hide the player from your monitor — viewers still see it in your stream.
